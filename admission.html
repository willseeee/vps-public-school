School Rules

Important guidelines for students and families.

General Rules

  • Maintain discipline inside and outside the school premises.
  • Minimum 75% attendance is compulsory.
  • Wear proper school uniform on all working days.
  • Bring required books and materials daily.
  • Mobile phones, valuables, and harmful materials are prohibited.
  • Take care of school property and maintain hygiene.

Fees Rules

  • Fees must be paid on or before the due date as notified by the school.
  • Late fee charges may apply after the due date.
  • Long pending dues may affect examination eligibility and continuation.
  • Fees once paid are generally non-refundable.
  • The school reserves the right to revise the fee structure when necessary.

Guidelines to Parents

  • Ensure regular attendance and punctuality.
  • Check homework, diary, and academic progress regularly.
  • Attend PTMs and communicate during working hours through proper channels.
  • Do not send prohibited items or an unwell child to school.
  • Support school discipline and encourage co-curricular participation.

Library Rules

  • Silence and discipline must be maintained in the library.
  • Books should be returned before the due date.
  • Damage or loss of books must be compensated.
  • Mobile phones, electronic devices, bags, and eatables are restricted.

Code of Conduct

  • Students must respect teachers, staff, and fellow students.
  • Bullying, harassment, cheating, and abusive language are prohibited.
  • Proper dress, punctuality, and academic honesty are expected.
  • Students should encourage harmony, cooperation, and social responsibility.

Discipline Policy

  • The policy promotes self-discipline, safety, and respect.
  • Misconduct may lead to verbal warning, written warning, suspension, or expulsion.
  • Parents and teachers play an active role in building positive behaviour.

Rules for Examination

  • Reach the examination hall on time with ID or admit card.
  • Bring your own exam materials.
  • Use of unfair means, phones, or smart devices is strictly prohibited.
  • Maintain silence and follow invigilator instructions throughout the exam.
  • Submit answer sheets properly before leaving the hall.
  • Absence without valid reason may result in zero marks.
