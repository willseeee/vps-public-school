General Rules

Varanasi Public School, Kerakatpur

1. Discipline

  • Maintain strict discipline inside and outside the premises.
  • Inappropriate language or behavior is strictly prohibited.

2. Attendance

  • Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory for academic eligibility.

3. School Uniform

  • Clean and complete uniform is required on all working days.

4. Academic Responsibility

  • Timely completion of homework and assignments is expected.

5. Prohibited Items

  • Mobile phones and valuable items are strictly restricted.

6. School Property

  • Students must act as guardians of school property.

7. Cleanliness

  • Hygiene and campus cleanliness must be maintained by all.

Fees Rules

1. Payment Schedule

  • Fees must be paid by the due date to avoid penalties.

2. Late Fees

  • Late charges apply to payments made after the deadline.

3. Refund Policy

  • Institutional fees are non-refundable.

Guidelines to Parents

1. Regularity

  • Ensure your child’s punctual attendance and academic discipline.

2. Communication

  • Attend Parent-Teacher Meetings regularly.

Library Rules

1. Discipline

  • Strict silence is mandatory in the library zone.

2. Book Care

  • Handle library resources with extreme care.

Code of Conduct

1. Integrity

  • Students are expected to uphold the highest moral standards.

2. Respect

  • Mutual respect among students and staff is a core value.

Discipline Policy

Our discipline policy is corrective, not punitive. We aim to foster self-regulation and respect for the learning environment.

Examination Rules

Conduct

  • Strict adherence to time and ID card rules.
  • Zero tolerance for unfair means.

Attendance

  • Valid medical reasons are required for missed exams.
