A Co-Educational Senior Secondary School, Affiliated to CBSE (5+3+3+4), New Delhi

Where learning grows into confidence, character, and service.

Varanasi Public School is the culmination of the vision, inspiration, and zeal of the members of Varanasi Shiksha Samiti, Varanasi. Since receiving CBSE affiliation in January 2005, VPS has continued its journey with academic commitment, values-based education, and a strong focus on all-round development.
