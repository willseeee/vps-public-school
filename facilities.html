VPS Kerakatpur - Academic Facilities

VPS is committed to delivering a wholesome academic environment with open learning spaces, classroom support, stream options at senior secondary level, and opportunities for practical learning.

Library

The library supports reading, reference work, and academic development with books from multiple streams of knowledge and a disciplined reading atmosphere.

Smart Class Room

Technology-aided teaching helps students understand topics through visual and audio support, making lessons clearer and more engaging.

Computer Laboratory

A well-equipped computer lab helps students learn digital skills with both practical and theoretical guidance.

Science Laboratory

Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories promote curiosity, experimentation, and scientific temperament.

Activity Rooms

Activity-based learning spaces allow younger students to learn through guided play, interaction, and creative exploration.

Art Room

The art room gives students space to explore imagination, color, craft, and visual expression under guidance.

Math Lab

The math lab supports concept-based learning and makes abstract mathematical ideas easier to understand.

Music & Dance

Dedicated music and dance spaces support performance, expression, rhythm, movement, and confidence building.

Playground / Sports

Sports and open spaces help students develop teamwork, discipline, physical fitness, and confidence.

Meeting Hall

A shared venue for school discussions, group sessions, presentations, and coordinated activities.

Open Air Theatre

The open air theatre creates opportunities for stage exposure, performance, and personality development.

Sports Facilities

Indoor and outdoor sports support regular training, active participation, and identification of student talent.

Socially Useful Productive Work

SUPW activities encourage community thinking, practical problem-solving, responsibility, and a scientific outlook.
