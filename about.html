About the School

Varanasi Public School (VPS), located in Kerakatpur, Lohta Road, Varanasi, is a beacon of educational excellence and holistic growth.

Established in 2003, VPS is a co-educational institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). We offer a comprehensive educational journey from Nursery to Class 12, featuring specialized streams in Science (PCM & PCB), Commerce, and Arts.

Managed by the Varanasi Shiksha Samiti, our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds through academic rigor, moral values, and diverse co-curricular opportunities. Our campus is a vibrant ecosystem of learning, equipped with modern laboratories, a rich library, and expansive sports facilities.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To create a progressive and inclusive educational environment where students think independently, develop leadership, and grow into responsible global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality CBSE-based education with innovative teaching methods.
  • Foster academic, moral, and social development in every child.
  • Ensure a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment.
  • Inculcate values of integrity, tolerance, and civic responsibility.

Manager Message

Welcome to Varanasi Public School. Here, education transcends traditional boundaries to shape individuals who are not just knowledgeable, but wise and compassionate.

Our commitment at VPS Kerakatpur is to provide a nurturing environment where students can flourish. We believe in instilling a passion for lifelong learning and the courage to innovate.

Mrs. Divya Pandey

Principal Message

Education is the ultimate liberation. At VPS, we don't just build classrooms; we build characters and experiences that define lifetimes.

VPS Society

The Varanasi Shiksha Samiti is the pillar of strength for Varanasi Public School. Dedicated to the cause of quality education, the Samiti ensures that the school remains a place of modern learning rooted in traditional values.

Founded by the visionary Shri S. N. Pandey, the Samiti continues to guide the institution toward new horizons of excellence, ensuring a legacy of character-building and academic success.
