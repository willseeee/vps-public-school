About the School

Varanasi Public School (VPS), located in Kerakatpur, Lohta Road, Varanasi-221107, is a co-educational institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). Established in 2003, VPS offers education from Nursery to Class 12, with streams in Science (PCM & PCB), Commerce, and Arts.

The school is managed by the Varanasi Shiksha Samiti and is dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience. VPS focuses on academic excellence, co-curricular activities, and the overall development of students. The campus features modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, a library, and opportunities for indoor and outdoor sports.

Mission & Vision

Vision

To create a progressive and inclusive educational environment where students think independently, develop leadership, and grow into responsible global citizens.

Mission

  • Provide quality CBSE-based education with modern teaching methods.
  • Support academic, moral, and social development of every child.
  • Create a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment.
  • Promote values like integrity, tolerance, and responsibility.

Manager Message

Welcome to Varanasi Public School, Kerakatpur, where education goes beyond textbooks to shape responsible, confident, and capable individuals. Our institution is committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can develop academically, socially, and morally.

At VPS Kerakatpur, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge but also about instilling values, discipline, and a passion for lifelong learning. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create an atmosphere that encourages innovation, curiosity, and excellence.

We take pride in offering a balanced curriculum that integrates academics, co-curricular activities, and character-building programs. Our mission is to prepare students to face the challenges of the future with confidence and integrity.

Mrs. Divya Pandey
Manager, Varanasi Public Group of Education

Principal Message

Sa Vidya Ya Vimuktaye - Education is that which liberates.

Education is about awakening the power and beauty that lies within all of us. To our school, education is not just about bricks and classrooms but about building character, enriching minds, and creating experiences that last a lifetime.

VPS aims to empower students with creativity, observation, and knowledge so they become excellent citizens with strong character. Our dream is to help every child grow as an academically strong, emotionally balanced, and socially responsible individual.

We provide a healthy and safe environment where the school becomes a home away from home. Skilled and dedicated teachers use innovative techniques, rich resources, and engaging programs to help students develop linguistic, mathematical, scientific, artistic, physical, emotional, and social skills.

Mrs. Priyanka Singh
Principal, Varanasi Public School

VPS Society

Varanasi Shiksha Samiti is the esteemed managing body behind Varanasi Public School. The Samiti is dedicated to promoting quality education and nurturing young minds with strong moral values and modern knowledge.

Established with a vision to uplift educational standards in society, the Samiti has played a vital role in the foundation and continuous development of the school. The institution reflects the dedication, vision, and commitment of its members towards excellence in education.

The foundation of the school under the guidance of the Samiti was laid by renowned educationist and social worker Shri S. N. Pandey, whose mission was to create an institution that blends academic excellence with character building.
